Privacy Policy for MMLottery

Effective Date: October 14, 2024

At MMLottery, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information about you when you use our mobile application, including the use of third-party services like AdMob, AppLovin, and Firebase Authentication.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number when provided by the user during account creation (via Firebase Authentication).
  • Usage Data: We may collect information on how the app is accessed and used, such as your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, version, and the pages of our app that you visit.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you are using, including the device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services.
  • To improve the performance and functionality of the app.
  • To serve personalized ads through AdMob and AppLovin based on your usage patterns.
  • To authenticate users through Firebase Authentication.
  • To ensure the security of the app and our services.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to enhance your experience:

  • AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display ads within the app. AdMob may collect device information, IP address, and usage data to serve personalized ads. You can learn more about Google’s privacy practices here.
  • AppLovin: AppLovin serves personalized advertisements in our app. They may collect data such as device identifiers and location data. You can learn more about AppLovin’s privacy practices here.
  • Firebase Authentication: We use Firebase Authentication to manage user sign-ins. Firebase may collect information like email addresses or phone numbers to provide its service. You can learn more about Firebase’s privacy practices here.
  • Firebase Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to track user behavior and app performance. Firebase Analytics collects usage data to help us improve the app.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • With third-party service providers who assist in operating the app (e.g., analytics and ad services).

5. Security

We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Effective Date" will be updated accordingly.
